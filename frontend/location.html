Getting Here

Multiple convenient ways to reach Urban Oasis from anywhere in Killeen or beyond.

By Car

From Fort Cavazos:
Take US-190 West to North College Street. Urban Oasis is located on North College Street. Approximately 10 minutes from Fort Cavazos gates.

From Downtown:
Head north on College Street for approximately 1 mile. We're on the right side of the street with ample parking available.

By Taxi/Ride Share

Uber and Lyft are available throughout Killeen. Use our address in your ride-share app for direct navigation to Urban Oasis. Typically $5-15 from downtown or Fort Cavazos.

By Public Transit

Local bus services are available throughout Killeen. Check with Killeen Transit for routes and schedules to North College Street. Several routes serve our area.

Walking

Urban Oasis is located in a walkable area of Killeen. Many downtown shops and restaurants are within 1-2 blocks. Safe, well-lit streets for pedestrians.

About Killeen & the Area

Welcome to Killeen, Texas

Killeen is a vibrant city in Central Texas with a rich military heritage and a diverse, welcoming community. Home to Fort Cavazos, one of the largest military installations in the United States, Killeen has become an important business and leisure destination.

Why Choose Killeen?

  • Strategic Location: Central hub for business and military personnel with easy access to Dallas, Austin, and Waco
  • Diverse Dining: Authentic international cuisine alongside popular American restaurants and cafés
  • Entertainment: Year-round events, concerts, theater productions, and cultural activities
  • Outdoor Recreation: Parks, trails, and recreational facilities for outdoor enthusiasts
  • Shopping: Multiple shopping centers and retail districts with major and local brands
  • Community: Welcoming neighborhoods with excellent hospitality and services
